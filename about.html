Our Story

A personal approach to professional renovation.

Iron Oak Renovations is a London, Ontario renovation company committed to delivering quality workmanship, dependable communication and a professional experience from the first conversation to the final walkthrough.

We understand that a renovation is more than a construction project. It is an investment in your home and a disruption to your everyday life. That is why we take the time to understand your goals, explain the process clearly and coordinate the details carefully.

We use licensed trades, carry full insurance and make sure our trades are WSIB compliant. Every project is backed by a 3-year workmanship warranty because we believe our responsibility continues after the work is complete.
